My Interests

Gaming

I like to play games during my free time because it helps me relax and have fun, especially after a long day of studying. Playing games allows me to escape from stress for a while and enjoy exciting challenges, whether I'm playing on my phone, computer, or with friends. It also helps improve my focus, problem solving skills, and reaction time. Gaming is not just entertaining but it’s also a great way for me to unwind and enjoy myself.

Badminton

I like to play badminton because it’s a fun and active sport that I can enjoy with my family. It’s something we all like to do together, which makes it even more special. Playing badminton not only helps me stay healthy and active, but it also gives me the chance to spend quality time with the people I love. Whether we’re playing seriously or just for fun, I always enjoy the laughter, teamwork, and good memories we create on the court.

Photography

I like to take pictures of the view, especially when I’m surrounded by beautiful scenery like mountains, the beach, or a colorful sunset. Capturing these moments with my camera or phone helps me remember the peaceful feeling I had at that time. It’s also a fun and relaxing way to enjoy nature and express my creativity. Taking photos of the view makes me feel calm and happy, and I love looking back at them later as memories.
