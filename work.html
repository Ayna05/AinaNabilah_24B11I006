Adobe Illustrator

Throughout my studies and personal projects, I have used this software to produce clean and professional graphics. I am also proficient in using various tools such as the pen tool, layers, gradients, and typography to enhance the quality of my designs. Adobe Illustrator is one of my main tools for producing creative and high-quality artwork.

Adobe Animate

Although I don’t use Adobe Animate regularly, I have some basic experience with the software, particularly in creating simple animations and understanding the timeline and keyframe functions. I’ve explored features like character motion, frame-by-frame animation, and symbol usage during a few class projects or practice sessions. While it’s not my main design tool, I’m familiar with how Adobe Animate works and am open to improving my skills in animation and interactive design.

Adobe Photoshop

I have experience using Adobe Photoshop to edit and enhance images, create digital artwork, and design visual content for various purposes. I am skilled in basic photo editing techniques such as cropping, retouching, color correction, and using adjustment layers. I also have knowledge in creating layouts for posters, social media graphics, and digital collages. Through my studies and personal practice, I’ve become comfortable working with tools like layer masks, brushes, and blending modes to produce clean and creative designs.
