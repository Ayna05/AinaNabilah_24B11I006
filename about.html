About Me

My name is Aina Nabilah. I am a student at UC TATI studying art and design. I have always enjoyed being creative but I have a passion for any element of creativity sketching, designing, and brainstorming ideas. Art is not simply a subject for me, it is something I really enjoy and feel connected to. I am always finding styles and ideas to explore and learning different ways to express myself through my work. Good design should tell a story, this is what I am trying to do in my work.

Liked

Thing that I like

There are many things that I like to do in my free time, especially those that let me express my creativity.

Liked

1 - WORD SEARCH

In my free time I enjoy doing word search puzzles, especially after a long day of classes, as I believe it is a form of relaxation and stress relief for me. It’s an unburdened and pleasant thing for me to do that brings calm and happiness a relaxing respite from the pace and turbulence of everyday life, and it’s become one of my favorite ways to wind down.

2 - Going to the Beach

Going to the beach is what I prefer to do in my free time and especially after a long and tiring day of lectures because it helps me destress and relax my mind. Listening to the waves crashing, the sand under my feet, and the cool breeze from the sea always calms me. My visits to the beach grant me the oppurtunity to breathe nature, reflect, and recharge which makes this spot an amazing place to rejuvenate and escape from my hectic life.

3 - Spending time with my family

I really enjoy spending my free time with my family. It’s a wonderful opportunity for us to connect, make joyful memories, and simply enjoy being together. Whether we’re out for a meal, shopping, or just taking a leisurely stroll, I always feel so relaxed and happy in their company. Those moments help me forget about the stress from school and remind me just how important it is to appreciate the people I love.
